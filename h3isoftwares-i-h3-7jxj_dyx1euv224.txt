<ul>
<li><a href="https://cloud.r-project.org/index.html" target="_blank">R</a>

<i>jamovi is a new “3rd generation” statistical spreadsheet. designed from the ground up to be easy to use, jamovi is a compelling alternative to costly statistical products such as SPSS and SAS. jamovi is built on top of the R statistical language, giving you access to the best the statistics community has to offer. would you like the R code for your analyses? jamovi can provide that too. jamovi will always be free and open - that's one of our core values - because jamovi is made by the scientific community, for the scientific community.</i> [Informações do <i>site</i> do jamovi em 27 mar. 2024.]</li>
